#  Car Sales Analysis – Tableau Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing car sales data to identify trends in revenue, sales volume, and customer preferences. An interactive **Tableau dashboard** was developed to provide business insights through KPIs, filters, and visual storytelling.

---

##  Objectives
- Analyze year-to-date (YTD) car sales performance  
- Track key metrics such as **total sales, average price, cars sold, and YoY growth**  
- Identify trends across **body style, color, transmission type, and dealer region**  
- Enable data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations  

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- **Tableau** – Dashboard design, calculated fields, filters, and interactivity  
- **Microsoft Excel / CSV** – Data preprocessing and validation  

---

##  Key KPIs Analyzed
- Total Sales  
- Average Car Price  
- Total Cars Sold  
- Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth  

---

##  Dashboard Features
- KPI cards for high-level performance monitoring  
- Trend analysis of sales over time  
- Regional analysis by **dealer region**  
- Segmentation by **body style, car color, and transmission**  
- Dynamic filters for interactive exploration  

---

##  Insights Generated
- Identified top-performing car body styles and regions  
- Analyzed customer preferences based on color and transmission type  
- Highlighted sales growth patterns and seasonal trends  
- Enabled stakeholders to drill down into specific segments for deeper insights
